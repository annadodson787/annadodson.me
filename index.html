Student: About Me

My name is Anna Dodson, and I am a student at Dartmouth College. I'm from Portland, Oregon, and I'm majoring in Computer Science and Engineering modified with Neuroscience. I enjoy creating and learning within a multitude of fields, including computer science, robotics, education, and neuroscience. When I'm not applying myself to my education, my WISP internship for DALI, or Dartmouth Humanitarian Engineering, I enjoy singing, skiing, horseback riding, and writing novels.

Inventor: My Innovation

From designing robots to humanitarian devices to apps, I basically live by the design process. Check out some of the stuff I've made:

  • Surplus: An app still in the works, built though the WISP program at Dartmouth. I built the backend using Firebase, incorporating realtime database, storage, and authentication.
  • HoneyHunter: An independent pygame project I built for CodeDay Portland.
  • ScumBot: An aquatic robot designed to remove invasive species such as Duckweed from the surface of ponds.
  • Water Trotter: A sustainable water purification and transportation mechanism, reusing old tires and Camelbak UV lights.
